About BOFF

We are organizators of the Brooklyn outdoor film festival which will take place from 5th till 8th of August 2018 at the Brooklyn Bridge park. We will play movies every day from 6pm till midnight. Tickets are free for everyone and all filmlovers are invited. We will play movies every 2 hours. First movie starts at 6pm, second one at 8pm and the last one starts at 10pm.

The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival, is an International, competitive festival. BOFF mission is to provide a public forum in Brooklyn in order to advance public interest in films and the independent production of films. To draw worldwide attention to Brooklyn as a center for cinema.
